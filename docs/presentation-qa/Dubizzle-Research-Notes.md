# Dubizzle Research and Lessons for BSR Hub

## What BSR Hub Can Learn

Based on Dubizzle's official Help Center, useful trust and safety patterns include:

- encourage email and phone verification;
- review or moderate listings;
- allow users to report suspicious listings and accounts;
- allow blocking abusive users;
- encourage communication inside the platform;
- hide personal phone numbers from public listings;
- warn users about phishing links and fake courier messages;
- prohibit dangerous, illegal, counterfeit, financial, and other restricted products;
- suspend or ban accounts suspected of spam, prohibited activity, scams, or fraud;
- show a verification badge for verified people or businesses;
- give sellers listing insights and encourage complete descriptions and photos.

## Important Difference

Dubizzle's official safety documentation says Dubizzle generally operates as a marketplace and does not participate in buyer–seller payments. It specifically warns users about fake messages claiming that Dubizzle or a courier has collected payment.

Therefore:

- BSR Hub may learn from Dubizzle's verification, reporting, moderation, privacy, and anti-phishing practices.
- BSR Hub must not claim that its delayed seller payout is copied from Dubizzle.
- If BSR Hub later handles marketplace payments, it should use a regulated provider such as Stripe Connect and obtain legal/payment advice before launch.
- The two-week MVP uses Stripe test mode and only simulates payment, refund, deposit, and delayed payout states.

## Features to Consider After the MVP

1. Verified user and verified business badges.
2. Built-in chat that warns against external payment links.
3. Report listing, report user, and block user actions.
4. Listing review queue and prohibited-items checks.
5. Seller response time, completion rate, cancellation rate, and rating.
6. Listing insights: views, saves, inquiries, and completed orders.
7. Safety center explaining phishing, fake courier links, and private-data protection.
8. Graduated enforcement: warning, listing removal, temporary suspension, permanent ban.
