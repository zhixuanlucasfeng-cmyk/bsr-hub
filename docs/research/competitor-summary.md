# Lucian Competitor Research Summary

Reviewed July 14, 2026. These are product-pattern notes, not copied marketing text.

| Service | Pattern reviewed | BSR Hub lesson | Risk to avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Fat Llama](https://fatllama.com/) | Peer-to-peer equipment rental with identity, pricing, availability, and handoff expectations | Make condition, included accessories, dates, and owner rules explicit | A broad catalog without local supply feels empty |
| [ShareGrid](https://www.sharegrid.com/) | Camera and production-equipment rentals | Use category-specific fields and strong condition photos | Complex professional insurance is too heavy for a two-week MVP |
| [Peerspace](https://www.peerspace.com/) | Hourly creative and event-space booking | Workspaces must be used on site and need capacity/amenity details | Never show a private exact address before a protected booking |
| [eBay Seller Center](https://www.ebay.com/sellercenter/listings/create-listings) | Condition, photos, item specifics, comparable-price research, seller protection | Explain condition and recommend prices from comparable attributes | Weak condition descriptions increase disputes and returns |
| [Taskrabbit](https://www.taskrabbit.com/services/shopping-delivery) | Local task and delivery coordination | Treat delivery as a separate paid service with status and responsibility | Delivery cannot be promised for immovable workspaces |
| [Facebook Marketplace](https://www.facebook.com/marketplace/) | Local discovery and direct seller communication | Local search and low-friction listing creation drive supply | Off-platform payment and messaging reduce protection |

## Recommended MVP principles

1. Keep payments protected inside BSR Hub until the transaction finishes.
2. Show city/state publicly and reveal precise handoff details only to participants.
3. Use deposits, condition evidence, verified identities, reviews, and an audit trail together.
4. Recommend a price but allow a small seller-controlled ±$5 adjustment.
5. Distinguish movable delivery, owner-location use, pickup, and immovable on-site booking.
