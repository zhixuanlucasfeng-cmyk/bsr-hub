# Pricing, Privacy, and Marketplace Notes

- PS5 recommendations consider model, age, condition, missing features, and cleanliness.
- Workspace recommendations consider square footage, facility type, condition, amenities, and location tier.
- All rentals have a 30-minute minimum. Partial billing units round up.
- BSR Hub adds a visible 6% service fee; delivery and refundable deposit remain separate line items.
- The seller may adjust the recommendation by no more than $5 per billing unit.
- Demo listings use only fictional descriptions and approximate Massachusetts cities. No phone, precise address, identity document, or payment credential is included.
- Future high-risk areas are fraud, chargebacks, damaged inventory, unsafe meetings, prohibited items, discriminatory listing behavior, and delivery disputes.
