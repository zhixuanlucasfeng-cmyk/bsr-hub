# BSR Logo Design

## Purpose

Create one simple, recognizable identity shared by BSR Hub and BSR Runner. The logo must work in the website navigation, presentation slides, social avatars, and a future app icon.

## Approved Direction

Use a compact **shared-square** mark: a deep charcoal rounded square containing bold white `BSR` lettering and one small lime circular-arrow accent. The arrow represents renting, reselling, reuse, and products moving through the community. The mark must remain legible at favicon and mobile-navigation sizes.

## Visual System

- Primary background: deep charcoal, matching the current website header and footer.
- Primary lettering: white, bold geometric sans serif.
- Action accent: BSR lime, matching the website's trust badges and buttons.
- Optional secondary accent: BSR purple, used only in the wordmark separator or a very small detail.
- Flat vector-friendly construction with no gradients, shadows, photographic texture, mascots, or detailed illustrations.
- Rounded corners should feel friendly and modern without looking childish.

## Lockups

1. **Primary horizontal lockup:** square `BSR` mark followed by `BSR Hub` in dark text.
2. **Runner lockup:** the same square mark followed by `BSR Runner`.
3. **Icon-only version:** the square `BSR` mark for app icons, favicons, and social avatars.

The letterforms and mark must remain identical across Hub and Runner. Only the product name changes.

## Generation Requirements

- Generate a clean presentation sheet showing the primary Hub lockup, Runner lockup, and icon-only mark.
- Preserve the exact text: `BSR`, `BSR Hub`, and `BSR Runner`.
- Keep generous clear space around every version.
- Use a plain light neutral presentation background.
- Avoid mockups on phones, walls, clothing, or stationery; the first output should show the identity itself clearly.
- Avoid extra slogans, invented symbols, misspelled text, 3D effects, and excessive decorative elements.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The `BSR` letters are immediately readable at small size.
- The circular-arrow accent is visible but does not interfere with the letters.
- The Hub and Runner lockups visibly belong to one brand family.
- The design matches the existing BSR website palette and modern marketplace tone.
- The result is simple enough to recreate later as a production SVG.
