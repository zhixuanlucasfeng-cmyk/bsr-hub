# BSR Hub — Two-Minute Five-Person Presentation

## Nasia — Presenter

Hello everyone. We are BSR Hub. I am Nasia, our presenter. Many people need products or workspaces that they cannot afford to buy. At the same time, other people own useful resources that remain unused most of the time. Our goal is to bring these people together.

## Lucian — Researcher

I am Lucian, our researcher. Imagine a student who wants to play a PS5 game with friends but cannot spend hundreds of dollars on a console. A photographer may need a professional camera for one weekend, while a small clothing business may need a printing facility for only a few hours. Buying everything makes little financial sense.

## Anna — Communication

I am Anna, and I lead communication. BSR Hub connects people who need affordable access with owners who have unused resources. Owners can earn extra income while customers save money. My role is also to help our team communicate clearly and combine its research, business, design, and technology work.

## Yichen — Logic and Problem-Solving

I am Yichen, and I focus on logic and problem-solving. Users can rent consoles, computers, cameras, and tools; reserve studios, printing facilities, and small factories; or trade second-hand products. Each listing can provide on-site use, pickup, or paid delivery. Spaces that cannot be moved are reserved and used at their location.

## Lucas — Coding and Coordination

I am Lucas, the coder and project coordinator. I am turning our idea into a website where users can create accounts, publish listings, search, book, and make test payments.

BSR Hub supports UN Goal 8, Decent Work and Economic Growth, by helping owners earn income and businesses access resources. It supports Goal 10, Reduced Inequalities, by making products, technology, and workspaces affordable. Instead of requiring everyone to own everything, BSR Hub helps communities share what they already have. Thank you.
